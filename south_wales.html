USW
Made from more

The University of South Wales (USW) is more than just a university-it's a place where ambition meets opportunity! USW was formed in 2013 through the merger of the University of Glamorgan and University of Wales, Newport, creating one of the largest and most industry-focused universities in the UK. With a strong reputation for career-ready education, cutting-edge research, and strong industry links, USW ensures students graduate with real-world skills and global opportunities

What makes South Wales Special?

Career-Focused Courses:

USW offers industry-relevant courses designed with employability in mind. Many programs include work placements, internships, and real-world projects.

Affordable and Supportive Environment:

USW is known for its affordable tuition fees and cost of living compared to other UK universities, along with outstanding student support services.

Cutting-Edge Facilities:

USW boasts state-of-the-art simulation centers, Glm studios, flight simulators, and forensic science labs, offering hands-on learning experiences.

Thriving Student Life:

From sports teams to cultural societies and social events, USW offers a lively and diverse campus experience.
